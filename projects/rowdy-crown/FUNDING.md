## SBA Loans — CONFIRMED ELIGIBLE

Bars and nightclubs are **explicitly eligible** for SBA 7(a) and 504 loans.

### SBA 7(a) — PRIMARY RECOMMENDATION

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Max amount | $5,000,000 |
| SBA guarantee | 75–85% |
| Down payment | 10–20% |
| Rate (2026) | Prime + 2.75% (~10.5–11.25% variable) |
| Term (real estate) | Up to 25 years |
| Term (equipment/WC) | Up to 10 years |
| Use | Acquisition, renovation, FF&E, working capital |
| Apply via | SBA-approved lenders — Austin SBDC can refer |

**Contact:** Austin SBDC — https://www.austinsbdc.org | Free SBA loan guidance

### SBA 504 — FOR REAL ESTATE PURCHASE

Stack with SBA 7(a) if purchasing the building:
- 50% conventional lender
- 40% SBA 504 (fixed rate, long term)
- 10% borrower equity (Smith Capital)

**Best scenario:** Smith Capital buys 15119 N IH-35 via SBA 504 ($1.0–1.5M), Rowdy Crown LLC gets SBA 7(a) for buildout + working capital ($500K–$750K).
