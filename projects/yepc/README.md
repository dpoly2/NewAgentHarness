# Youth Elite Performance Complex (YEPC)

**Last Updated:** May 20, 2026
**Status:** Site analysis complete ✅ | Capital fundraising in progress 🔜

---

## Overview

The **Youth Elite Performance Complex (YEPC)** is a 110-acre mixed-use sports and community development project located in Hutto, TX, on the CR 132 parcel. The project is being developed under **Smith Capital Properties** and is designed to serve as a regional hub for youth athletic training, competition, and community enrichment.

## Opportunity Zone 2.0

Research indicates the immediate 110-acre CR 132 parcel likely does **not** qualify for OZ 1.0 due to median income thresholds. However, **Census Tract 208.08** (nearby) does qualify and represents a strategic land acquisition or partnership opportunity. OZ 2.0 community nomination deadline: **June 26, 2026**.
