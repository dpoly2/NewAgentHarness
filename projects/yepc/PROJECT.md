# Youth Elite Performance Complex (YEPC)
## Master Project File

---

## Project Overview
- **Project Name:** Youth Elite Performance Complex (YEPC)
- **Location:** CR 132 parcel, Hutto, TX (~110 acres)
- **Parcel:** ~110 acres adjoining Limmer Loop and SH-130
- **Developer:** Smith Capital Properties
- **Primary Org Beneficiary:** Xtreme Force Track Club (XFTC)
- **Status:** Pre-Development / Planning Phase
- **Target Full Buildout:** 2027 (aligned with SH-130 widening)

---

## Vision
A world-class youth sports and performance facility serving the Austin/Pflugerville/Hutto region — anchored by track & field and designed to host regional and national-level youth athletic competitions, training programs, and community events.

---

## Site Details
- **Address/Parcel:** CR 132, Hutto, TX (near Limmer Loop & SH-130)
- **Size:** ~110 acres
- **Topography:** Gently sloped, buildable
- **Key Infrastructure:**
  - SH-130 toll road (east boundary)
  - CR 132/134 arterial expansion planned (6-lane divided)
  - FM 3349 widening underway (4-lane divided)
  - Shared-use path along CR 132 in development
  - New detention pond near realignment zone

## Funding Strategy
- Federal: EDA Public Works grants, HUD CDBG, USATF facility grants
- State: Texas Economic Development, Texas Facilities Commission, TSAHC
- Local: Hutto EDC incentives, Williamson County development grants, TIF zones
- Private: Naming rights, corporate sponsorships, private investors
- Nonprofit: XFTC 501(c)(3) as programmatic anchor for grant eligibility
- Bonds/Lending: CDFI loans, SBA 504 (commercial real estate), tax-exempt bonds
