**SUBJECT:** Inquiry — Youth Elite Performance Complex | CR 132 Parcel | ~110 Acres

Dear Director Gamboa,

My name is David Smith, and I am the principal of Smith Capital Properties, a Texas-based real estate development firm. I am reaching out to introduce a significant development project we are actively planning in the City of Hutto and to explore available economic development incentives and partnership opportunities with the Hutto EDC.

**The Project — Youth Elite Performance Complex (YEPC)**

We are in the pre-development planning phase of the Youth Elite Performance Complex (YEPC), a world-class youth sports and athletic performance facility proposed for the ~110-acre CR 132 parcel (Williamson County Tax ID R020309) adjoining Limmer Loop and SH-130.

The YEPC is designed to serve the Austin/Pflugerville/Hutto region and will be developed in three phases through 2027, aligned with the planned SH-130 widening and CR 132/134 arterial improvements. Phase 3 envisions a facility capable of hosting events with 10,000+ attendees, including regional and national-level youth athletic competitions.

The project will be anchored programmatically by **Xtreme Force Track Club**, a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit serving youth ages 6–18 in the Austin/Pflugerville area, with a focus on low-income youth development through track and field athletics.

**Planned Amenities Include:**
- USATF-certified 400m competition track and field events venue
- Multi-sport fields (soccer, football, lacrosse)
- Indoor training facility
- Stadium seating (10,000+ capacity in Phase 3)
- Retail/concession village, hospitality partnership
- Pedestrian and bike connectivity along CR 132 arterial

**Economic Impact**
A project of this scale will generate significant economic activity for Hutto — construction jobs, permanent operations employment, hotel room nights, local restaurant and retail spend during events, and increased visibility for Hutto as a regional sports destination.

**What We're Seeking**
We would welcome a conversation with your office to discuss:
- Chapter 380 Economic Development Agreement eligibility
- Tax abatement or infrastructure cost-sharing opportunities
- TIF zone applicability for the CR 132 parcel
- Any EDC grants or programs available for sports/recreation facilities
- City of Hutto zoning pathway for a facility of this type

We are eager to be a long-term partner in Hutto's continued growth and would love to schedule a meeting at your earliest convenience.
