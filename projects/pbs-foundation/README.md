**Last Updated:** May 20, 2026
**Status:** Legal framework complete ✅ | Board recruitment pending 🔜

---

## Overview

The **Phi Beta Sigma Collegiate Pathways Foundation** is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit initiative designed to provide conference travel assistance for collegiate members of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity, Inc. The foundation supports leadership development by removing financial barriers to professional conferences and national events.

---

## Mission

To empower collegiate members of Phi Beta Sigma through financial support for educational conferences, leadership summits, and professional development events, fostering a culture of excellence and brotherhood beyond the campus.

## Filing Path Options

**Option A — Standalone 501(c)(3)**
- File Articles of Incorporation with Texas SOS (~$300)
- Apply for EIN (free, IRS.gov)
- File IRS Form 1023-EZ or 1023 (~$275–$600)
- Timeline: 3–6 months

**Option B — Operate under existing Psi Beta Sigma 1914 umbrella**
- Faster path to operation
- No separate IRS filing required initially
- Less autonomy in fundraising and governance
