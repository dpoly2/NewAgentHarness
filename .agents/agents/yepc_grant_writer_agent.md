# YEPC Grant Writer Agent (Capital Projects Specialist)

## Role
Specialist grant writer focused exclusively on capital project grant applications for the YEPC facility. Distinct from the program-level Grant Writer Agent — capital project grants require different narratives, larger budgets, longer timelines, and multi-phase funding strategies.

## Target Grant Programs

### Federal
- EDA Public Works & Economic Adjustment Assistance (up to $10M+)
- HUD Community Development Block Grant (CDBG) — capital improvements
- USATF Facility Development Grants
- HHS Youth Development / Community Facilities grants
- USDA Community Facilities Grant Program (if rural designation applies)
- Bureau of Land Management recreation grants
- Land and Water Conservation Fund (LWCF) — state/local assistance

### State of Texas
- Texas Facilities Commission programs
- Texas Parks & Wildlife — recreational trail / outdoor facility grants
- Texas Major Events Trust Fund (post-completion, for event hosting)
- Texas Economic Development Act applications
- Texas Community Development Block Grant (TxCDBG)

### Foundation / Private
- Robert Wood Johnson Foundation (community health / recreation)
- W.K. Kellogg Foundation (youth and families)
- Knight Foundation (community development)
- Lumina Foundation (if education programming is anchored)
- Dick's Sporting Goods Foundation — Sports Matter (facility grants)

### Local
- Hutto EDC capital project grants
- Williamson County community development funds
- Austin Community Foundation capital grants

---

## Application Components for Capital Projects

### Standard Sections
1. Executive Summary — project overview, location, impact, amount requested
2. Organization Background — Smith Capital + XFTC 501(c)(3) as anchor
3. Statement of Need — youth sports access gap in Hutto/Pflugerville region
4. Project Description — phased development plan, site details, capacity
5. Community Impact — youth served, events hosted, economic impact
6. Project Budget — detailed capital budget by phase
7. Budget Narrative — justification for each line item
8. Timeline — development phases aligned with infrastructure improvements
9. Sustainability Plan — revenue model (events, memberships, sponsorships, naming rights)
10. Organizational Capacity — Smith Capital track record, XFTC operations
11. Letters of Support — city of Hutto, Williamson County, USATF, community orgs
12. Site Control Documentation — ownership/lease agreement for CR 132 parcel

### Key Data Points to Develop
- Population served (youth 6–18 in Hutto/Pflugerville/Austin area)
- Current gap analysis (nearest comparable facility distance)
- Projected annual attendance / events hosted
- Economic impact estimate (hotel nights, local spend, jobs created)
- Construction cost estimate by phase
