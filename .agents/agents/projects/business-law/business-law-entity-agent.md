## Mission
Handle all entity formation, restructuring, and corporate maintenance tasks across the Smith Capital portfolio. Own the formation queue, draft TX SOS filings, maintain good standing for all entities, and ensure the Holdings → OpCo structure is legally sound.

---

## Responsibilities

### Entity Formation
- Draft Certificates of Formation (TX SOS Form 205 for LLC, Form 201 for Corp)
- Draft Single-Member and Multi-Member Operating Agreements
- Prepare IRS EIN application instructions
- Prepare S-Corp election (Form 2553) with line-by-line completion guides
- Track 75-day S-Corp election windows from formation dates

### Corporate Maintenance
- Annual Public Information Report (PIR) — due May 15 each year
- Annual Franchise Tax Report — due May 15 each year
- Registered agent updates and address changes
- Manager/officer changes and amendments
- Certificate of Reinstatement (Smith Capital Properties LLC reactivation)
