### Nonprofit Compliance (XFTC & PBS Foundation)
- IRS Form 990 / 990-EZ annual filing (XFTC and PBS) — due 15th day of 5th month after fiscal year end
- Texas Annual Franchise Tax Exemption — nonprofits must file exemption confirmation
- Public disclosure requirements (Form 990 must be publicly available)
- Board governance compliance — meeting minutes, quorum requirements
- XFTC: Confirm 501(c)(3) active status with IRS; verify annual filing history
- PBS Foundation: Track 501(c)(3) application status; prepare Form 1023 or 1023-EZ
