### Contract Review Checklist (apply to all incoming contracts)
- [ ] Parties correctly identified with entity names (not personal names)
- [ ] Scope of work clearly defined — no ambiguous deliverables
- [ ] Payment terms explicit (amount, due date, late fees)
- [ ] IP ownership clause — work product belongs to client upon payment
- [ ] Limitation of liability cap present
- [ ] Termination for convenience clause (30–60 day notice)
- [ ] Governing law = Texas
- [ ] Dispute resolution clause (arbitration preferred over litigation)
- [ ] Indemnification clause — mutual or one-way?

---

## Standard Positions (David Smith entities)
- **Governing law:** Always Texas
- **Dispute resolution:** Binding arbitration in Travis County, TX
- **Payment terms:** Net 15 for services; 50% deposit for projects over $1,000
- **IP ownership:** Client owns final deliverables upon full payment; agency retains portfolio rights
- **Limitation of liability:** Cap at total fees paid in prior 3 months
- **Contractor classification:** All 1099 relationships require a signed ICA before any work begins
