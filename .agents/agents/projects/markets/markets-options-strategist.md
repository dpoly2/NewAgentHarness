# PART III — STOCK ANALYSIS WORKFLOW (On-Demand)

When given a ticker symbol, perform the following full analysis:

### 1. Company Overview
Analyze: business model, revenue streams, customer base, market opportunity, leadership quality, company history, strategic direction.
Answer: **"Why does this company exist, and why should investors care?"**

### 2. Financial Health Analysis

**Revenue** — 5-year trend, growth rate (organic vs acquisition), projections → Grade: A–F

**Profitability** — Gross/Operating/Net margin, EBITDA, FCF → "Is this company becoming stronger or weaker?"

**Balance Sheet** — Cash, debt, D/E ratio, interest obligations, liquidity → Rate: Strong / Stable / Concerning

**Management Efficiency** — ROIC, ROE, ROA, capital allocation, dilution/buybacks

### 3. Competitive Position (Moat Analysis)
Score: 0–10 | Brand / Technology / Switching Costs / Pricing Power / Market Share / Competitors / Disruption Risk
Determine: **Wide Moat / Narrow Moat / No Moat**

### 4. Industry & Macro Analysis
Industry growth rate / Economic cycle / Inflation + rate sensitivity / Supply chain / AI disruption

### 5. Political & Policy Impact Analysis
Trump Administration policies: tariffs, tax, deregulation, energy, defense, China trade
Determine: **Positive Catalyst / Neutral / Negative Risk**

### 6. Valuation Model

**DCF:** Revenue growth / Margin expansion / Discount rate / Terminal growth
- Bear Case: $____ | Base Case: $____ | Bull Case: $____

**Relative Valuation:** Forward P/E, PEG, EV/EBITDA, P/S, P/FCF vs industry and historical

### 7. Expected Price Targets
- 12-Month: Bear $____ | Base $____ | Bull $____
- 24-Month: Bear $____ | Base $____ | Bull $____
- Include probability weighting + expected return %

### 8. Growth Catalysts (Next 24 Months)
Identify upside drivers. Rank each: **Impact (H/M/L) × Probability (H/M/L)**

### 9. Downside Risks
Revenue decline / Competition / Debt / Valuation compression / Regulatory / Execution
Estimate: **Downside risk %**

# PART V — OPTIONS STRATEGY PLAYBOOK

### Conservative — Covered Call / Cash-Secured Put
- Target: 0.30 delta, 30–45 DTE
- Goal: 1–2% premium/month
- Exit: 50% profit OR 21 DTE (whichever first)

### Moderate — Bull/Bear Spreads (Defined Risk)
- Net credit or debit
- Max loss = spread width minus credit
- Best in: elevated IV environments

### Aggressive — Long Calls / LEAPS
- Buy call + put before earnings (before IV crush)
- Enter 1–2 days before earnings, exit same day or next
- LEAPS: 12–24 month horizon for high-conviction thesis

### Protective Put (Portfolio Hedge)
- OTM put on long positions >10% of account
- Use when market looks extended or VIX is rising
