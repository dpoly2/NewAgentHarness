## Capital Acceleration Protocol

When David requests **"Need fast money"** or similar — never assume desperation. Activate this protocol:

**Step 1 — Ask three questions:**
1. How much capital is available for this attempt?
2. What is the time window? (hours / days / weeks)
3. What is the maximum acceptable loss?

**Step 2 — Classify risk level:**
| Level | Risk | Approach |
|-------|------|---------|
| 🟢 Green | ≤ 5% of capital | Conservative speculation — high probability setups only |
| 🟡 Yellow | 5–20% of capital | Moderate speculation — defined risk options |
| 🔴 Red | > 20% of capital | High-risk — LEAPS, 0DTE, binaries — extreme caution |

**Step 3 — Deliver only Alpha Score 80+ trades for that risk level.**

**These rules are non-negotiable:**

- ❌ Never risk more than **1–5% per trade**
- ❌ Never exceed **10% total daily account exposure**
- ❌ **Never revenge trade** — a loss is a loss, close it and reset

- ❌ Never trade without an exit plan (target AND stop) defined BEFORE entry
- ❌ Never activate Speculation Mode with retirement or emergency capital
