## 🏛️ FEDERAL GRANTS

### 1. EDA Public Works & Economic Adjustment Assistance
- **Amount:** $500K–$3M (up to 80% cost share)
- **Eligibility:** Projects creating jobs and economic development in distressed areas
- **Fit:** YEPC creates construction jobs, permanent staff, and drives regional sports tourism
- **Key angle:** Frame as a regional economic anchor — reference Samsung corridor and SH-130 widening
- **Apply via:** grants.gov | EDA Austin Regional Office
- **Deadline:** Rolling — next window typically Q1/Q3
- **Priority: HIGH**

### 2. HUD Community Development Block Grant (CDBG)
- **Amount:** $500K–$2M
- **Eligibility:** Low-to-moderate income (LMI) benefit — XFTC's youth athlete demographic qualifies
- **Fit:** XFTC serves low-income youth ages 6–18; YEPC would be anchor facility
- **Apply via:** Williamson County or City of Hutto (entitlement communities)
- **Note:** Work with Hutto EDC to include YEPC in their CDBG application
- **Priority: HIGH**

### 3. USATF Foundation — Facility Development Grants
- **Amount:** $25K–$250K
- **Eligibility:** USA Track & Field member clubs with certified facilities
- **Fit:** XFTC is a USATF-affiliated club; YEPC would host USATF-sanctioned meets
- **Apply via:** usatffoundation.org
- **Priority: MEDIUM**

### 4. Youth Sports Facilities Act (EDA — Bipartisan legislation)
- **Amount:** Up to $2M
- **Status:** Bill introduced in Congress to unlock EDA funding specifically for youth sports facilities in underserved areas
- **Fit:** Perfect alignment — monitor for passage and apply immediately if enacted
- **Priority: MONITOR**

### 5. FHWA Transportation Alternatives Program (TAP)
- **Amount:** $250K–$1M
- **Eligibility:** Pedestrian/bike connectivity, shared-use paths
- **Fit:** YEPC's planned shared-use path along CR 132 qualifies
- **Apply via:** TxDOT / CAMPO
- **Priority: MEDIUM**

## 🤠 STATE OF TEXAS GRANTS

### 6. Texas Parks & Wildlife — Community Outdoor Outreach Program (CO-OP)
- **Amount:** $5K–$100K
- **Eligibility:** Nonprofits serving underserved youth in outdoor/recreational settings
- **Fit:** XFTC 501(c)(3) as program anchor
- **Apply via:** tpwd.texas.gov
- **Deadline:** Annual — typically spring
- **Priority: MEDIUM**

### 7. Texas Economic Development — Major Events Trust Fund (METF)
- **Amount:** $500K–$5M (post-event reimbursement)
- **Eligibility:** Events generating significant hotel/sales tax revenue for the state
- **Fit:** YEPC hosting regional/national track & field championships qualifies
- **Apply via:** Texas Office of the Governor — Economic Development & Tourism
- **Note:** Must pre-certify events; works as a reimbursement after events are held
- **Priority: HIGH (Phase 2+)**

### 8. Comptroller's Texas Enterprise Fund (TEF)
- **Amount:** Negotiated (typically $500K–$5M)
- **Eligibility:** Projects creating significant jobs and capital investment in Texas
- **Fit:** YEPC Phase 3 hotel/commercial component + 200+ permanent jobs
- **Apply via:** Texas Governor's Office
- **Priority: HIGH (Phase 3)**

### 9. Opportunity Zone Tax Incentive
- **Amount:** Federal capital gains deferral/elimination (investor benefit)
- **Deadline:** State Opportunity Zone 2.0 nomination — **June 26, 2026**
- **Fit:** CR 132 Hutto parcel may qualify — confirm with Williamson County
- **Priority: URGENT — deadline in 35 days**

## 🏢 PRIVATE / CORPORATE SPONSORSHIPS

### 13. Facility Naming Rights
- **Target amount:** $2M–$5M (10-year deal)
- **Approach:** Pitch to regional anchors — Dell Technologies, Samsung Austin, Tesla Gigafactory (Taylor), Apple Campus (Austin)
- **Structure:** Main stadium naming ($3M), track naming ($1M), field naming ($500K each)
- **Deliverables:** Signage, media mentions, athlete/event exposure, community goodwill
- **Timeline:** Pitch during Phase 1 design; close before Phase 2 groundbreaking

### 14. Corporate Title Sponsorships
- **Target amount:** $500K–$1M/year
- **Prospects:**

  - Whataburger (Texas pride brand, youth sports sponsor)
  - Dell Technologies (Round Rock HQ, youth STEM/sports crossover)
  - Toyota (Georgetown TX plant, regional employer)

- **Structure:** Title = $1M/yr, Platinum = $500K/yr, Gold = $250K/yr, Silver = $100K/yr

### 15. Event Presenting Sponsorships
- **Target:** $50K–$250K per major event
- **Model:** "The [Sponsor Name] XFTC Invitational" — per-event deals that stack annually
- **Prospects:** Nike, Adidas, New Balance (all have youth track sponsorship programs)

### 16. Anchor Tenant / Hotel Partnership
- **Type:** Ground lease or JV

## 💰 DEBT / EQUITY FINANCING

### 17. SBA 504 Loan — Land Acquisition
- **Amount:** Up to $5.5M (SBA portion)
- **Terms:** 20–25 year fixed rate, ~6.5–7%
- **Fit:** Commercial real estate acquisition — YEPC LLC qualifies as for-profit entity
- **Pair with:** Smith Capital Properties equity contribution (10–20%)
- **Priority: HIGH — fund land acquisition in 2026**

### 18. CDFI Loan — Construction
- **Amount:** $1M–$5M
- **Lenders:** LiftFund, PeopleFund, Texas Community Capital
- **Terms:** Below-market rate, flexible underwriting for community-benefit projects
- **Priority: HIGH**

### 19. New Markets Tax Credits (NMTC)
- **Amount:** 39% tax credit on qualified investment (attracts private equity)
- **Fit:** If site is in a low-income census tract — confirm with Williamson County data
- **Structure:** Complex — use a CDFI intermediary (LiftFund, PeopleFund)
- **Priority: MEDIUM (Phase 2)**

### 20. Tax-Exempt Municipal Bonds
- **Amount:** $10M–$20M
- **Fit:** Phase 3 stadium component — City of Hutto or Williamson County issues bonds
- **Requires:** Strong public-private partnership agreement with Hutto
- **Priority: PHASE 3**
