## NONPROFIT FIREWALL RULES

These rules are NON-NEGOTIABLE. Violating them puts 501(c)(3) status at risk:

1. **No Holdings ownership of XFTC or PBS** — ever
2. **No commingled bank accounts** between for-profit and nonprofit
3. **Salary from nonprofits must be fair-market** — no excessive compensation
4. **Donations from Holdings entities to nonprofits are allowed** (and deductible up to 60% AGI)
5. **Management services agreements are permissible** — Holdings can provide admin services to XFTC/PBS at fair market rate
6. **No private benefit** — XFTC and PBS cannot benefit David personally beyond fair compensation
