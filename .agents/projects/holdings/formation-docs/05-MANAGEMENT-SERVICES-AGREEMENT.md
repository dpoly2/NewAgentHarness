## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Holdings is the sole member and parent company of the Company;

WHEREAS, Holdings has the expertise, infrastructure, and resources to provide management and administrative services that will benefit the Company;

WHEREAS, the Company desires to engage Holdings to provide such services in exchange for a monthly management fee at arm's-length fair market rates;

NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants set forth herein, the Parties agree as follows:

## ARTICLE 1 — SERVICES

**1.1 Scope of Services.** Holdings shall provide the following management and administrative services to the Company (the "Services"):

**a) Strategic Management**
- Long-range planning and goal-setting
- Business development strategy and direction
- Market positioning and competitive analysis
- Key decision support and executive advisory

**b) Financial Management**
- Oversight of budgets, forecasting, and cash flow planning
- Tax strategy coordination (in conjunction with CPA)
- Accounts payable/receivable oversight
- Quarterly financial review and reporting

**c) Legal & Compliance Coordination**
- Entity maintenance (annual PIR filings, franchise tax)
- Contract review and coordination with outside counsel
- Regulatory compliance monitoring
- License and permit renewal tracking

**d) Human Resources & Operations**
- Contractor/vendor onboarding and management
- Policy and procedure framework
- Operational systems and workflow design
- Technology infrastructure coordination

**e) Marketing & Brand Oversight**
- Brand standards enforcement
- Marketing strategy and campaign coordination (via S2T Designs, where applicable)
- Digital presence and social media direction

**f) Additional Services**
Holdings may provide additional services as mutually agreed in writing between the Parties.

## ARTICLE 2 — MANAGEMENT FEE

**2.1 Monthly Fee.** In consideration of the Services, the Company shall pay Holdings a monthly management fee of:

**$[MONTHLY FEE]** per month

- *Clarity Solar Services LLC: $500/mo*

**2.2 Payment Terms.** The management fee shall be paid by the 15th of each calendar month via bank transfer from the Company's business account to Holdings' business account.

**2.3 Fee Adjustments.** The monthly fee may be adjusted annually upon 30 days' written notice from Holdings. Adjustments shall reflect fair market rates for comparable services.

**2.4 Arm's-Length Rate.** The Parties acknowledge that the management fee is set at an arm's-length fair market rate, consistent with rates charged by third-party management companies for comparable services. This rate has been determined with reference to comparable market data.

**2.5 Commencement.** Fees shall begin accruing on the Effective Date. Holdings may defer collection of fees until the Company has sufficient cash flow, provided fees are tracked as a payable on the Company's books.

---

## ARTICLE 3 — TERM & TERMINATION

**3.1 Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial term of one (1) year, automatically renewing for successive one-year terms unless terminated.

**3.2 Termination for Convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon 60 days' written notice to the other Party.

**3.3 Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate immediately upon material breach, provided the breaching Party is given 15 days' written notice and opportunity to cure.

**3.4 Effect of Termination.** Upon termination, the Company shall pay all outstanding management fees within 30 days. Holdings shall provide reasonable transition assistance.

---

## ARTICLE 4 — RELATIONSHIP OF THE PARTIES

**4.1 Independent Relationship.** Holdings provides services as an independent management company. Nothing herein shall create an employment relationship between Holdings and the Company's employees or contractors.

**4.2 Parent-Subsidiary.** The Parties acknowledge that Holdings is the sole member of the Company. This Agreement is entered into on an arm's-length basis to properly document and compensate the management services provided.

**4.3 No Conflict.** Holdings may provide similar services to other affiliated entities simultaneously. Holdings shall not allow such other engagements to materially interfere with services to the Company.

---

## ARTICLE 5 — CONFIDENTIALITY

**5.1** Each Party shall keep confidential all proprietary business information, financial data, client lists, and trade secrets of the other Party.

**5.2** Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement for a period of three (3) years.

---

## ARTICLE 6 — LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

**6.1** Holdings' liability to the Company under this Agreement shall not exceed the total management fees paid in the three (3) months preceding the claim.

**6.2** Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages.
