## Subcontractor Agreement — Key Terms to Negotiate

When Hert sends over their subcontract, review and confirm these items:

### Financial Terms
| Term | What to Ask For | Acceptable Range |
|------|----------------|-----------------|
| Payment terms | Net-15 | Net-15 to Net-30 |
| Invoice format | Email PDF | Their preferred format |
| Billing trigger | Job completion + sign-off | Completion report submitted |
| Change order process | Written approval before work | Standard |
| Retainage | Zero (repair work, not construction) | 0–5% max |

### Insurance Requirements (Get in Writing)
- [ ] Minimum GL limits required
- [ ] Additional insured language
- [ ] Waiver of subrogation required?
- [ ] Workers' comp required?
- [ ] Umbrella minimum?
- [ ] E&O required?
- [ ] COI format and delivery method

### Scope & Operations
- [ ] What types of commercial repair will be routed to us?
- [ ] What is typical project size ($/job)?
- [ ] What is typical monthly volume (jobs/month)?
- [ ] What geographic area is covered?
- [ ] Who is our point of contact for dispatch?
- [ ] What is the dispatch/response time expectation?
- [ ] Do they provide equipment/parts or does Apex source?
- [ ] Who handles permitting on commercial jobs?

### Legal Protections (Push For These)
- [ ] Indemnification — mutual, not one-sided
- [ ] Limitation of liability — cap at contract value
- [ ] No exclusivity — Apex retains right to work with other commercial clients
- [ ] Termination — 30-day notice, not at-will
- [ ] Dispute resolution — mediation before litigation
