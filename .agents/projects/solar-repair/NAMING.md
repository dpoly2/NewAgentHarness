# Solar Repair Company — Name Analysis & Branding Recommendations
**Created:** 2026-05-24
**Managed By:** solar-marketing-agent
**Decision Needed From:** David Smith

## Current Name Assessment

**Smith and Taylor Construction** — Not recommended for this venture:
- "Construction" signals new builds, not repair/maintenance — wrong market signal
- Generic — doesn't differentiate in the solar space
- No solar/energy association — misses SEO opportunity
- Doesn't scale if David becomes the sole operator (removes "Taylor")
- Naming it after people limits future sale or partnership value

---

## Name Recommendations

### 🥇 TOP PICK — Apex Solar Services
**Why:** "Apex" signals top-tier, authoritative service. Clean, professional, scalable. Works for both residential homeowners and commercial clients (Hert contract). Strong domain potential. Easy to say, easy to spell, hard to confuse.
- **Domain target:** apexsolarservices.com / apexsolar.services
- **Tagline idea:** *"Peak Performance for Your Solar System"*
- **Brand feel:** Navy + orange, bold, trustworthy contractor energy
- **B2B fit:** Strong — Hert Renewables would feel confident listing Apex as a partner
- **Google search advantage:** "Apex Solar" + city name would rank well locally

---

### 🥈 RUNNER UP — Clarity Solar
**Why:** "Clarity" speaks to transparency, trust, and the literal goal of solar — maximum light capture. Resonates with homeowners who've had bad experiences with pushy installers. Premium feel.
- **Domain target:** claritysolar.com / claritysolarservices.com
- **Tagline idea:** *"Clear Skies. Clear Results."*
- **Brand feel:** Sky blue + white, clean, modern, premium
- **B2B fit:** Good — reads professional, not trade-heavy
- **Note:** Check trademark — "Clarity Solar" may have some existing use

---

### 🥉 STRONG CONTENDER — SunPath Services
**Why:** "SunPath" evokes the solar journey, warmth, and Texas outdoor culture. "Services" keeps it broad enough to expand. Friendly for residential customers. Good local brand.
- **Domain target:** sunpathservices.com / sunpathsolar.com
- **Tagline idea:** *"Follow the Sun. Fix the Rest."*
- **Brand feel:** Warm gold + dark green, Texas-friendly, community vibe
- **B2B fit:** Moderate — more consumer-facing than B2B
- **SEO advantage:** "SunPath" is distinctive enough to own the SERP quickly

---

### CREATIVE OPTION — Volt & Ray
**Why:** Clever dual meaning — "volt" (electrical) + "ray" (sunlight). Memorable, brandable, unique. Would stand out in a sea of "[City] Solar" names. Good for social media.
- **Domain target:** voltandray.com
- **Tagline idea:** *"Where Electrical Meets Solar"*
- **Brand feel:** Yellow + dark charcoal, bold, tech-forward
- **B2B fit:** Moderate — creative, but Hert may prefer something more traditional
- **Risk:** More memorable but may not immediately signal "solar repair" to searchers

---

### PREMIUM/ENTERPRISE — Keystone Solar Services
**Why:** "Keystone" = the essential piece that holds everything together. Perfect metaphor for repair/maintenance. Strong B2B energy — Hert Renewables would see this as a serious partner.
- **Domain target:** keystonesolarservices.com
- **Tagline idea:** *"The Essential Solar Partner"*
- **Brand feel:** Slate gray + yellow-gold, authoritative, contractor-grade
- **B2B fit:** Excellent — purpose-built for commercial/B2B positioning
- **Note:** Slightly longer — less punchy for residential

---

### REGIONAL PLAY — Summit Solar Repair
**Why:** "Summit" signals achievement + elevation (Texas hill country nod). "Repair" in the name is bold — most companies avoid it, but it's exactly what the search intent is. High SEO value.
- **Domain target:** summitsolarrepair.com
- **Tagline idea:** *"Repair. Restore. Perform."*
- **Brand feel:** Forest green + white, outdoorsy, dependable
- **B2B fit:** Good
- **SEO note:** Having "repair" in the name is a direct keyword match for "solar repair [city]" searches
