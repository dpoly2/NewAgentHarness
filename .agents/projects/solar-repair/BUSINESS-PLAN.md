## Executive Summary

A Texas-based solar repair and maintenance company serving residential homeowners (primary) and commercial clients via a subcontract relationship with Hert Renewables. The company fills a growing aftermarket gap in the Central Texas solar ecosystem — as over 1.5 million Texas residential systems age past their installation warranties, repair specialists are in high demand with minimal competition.

**Founder:** David Smith — Smith Capital Properties portfolio
**Headquarters:** Pflugerville, TX
**Launch Target:** Q3 2026

---

## Market Opportunity

| Stat | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Texas solar systems installed | 1.5M+ residential, growing 20%/yr |
| Average system age triggering repairs | 5–10 years post-install |
| Central TX systems in repair window | 100,000+ (est.) |
| Average residential repair ticket | $350–$2,500 |
| Annual maintenance contract | $200–$500/system/yr |
| Commercial repair (via Hert) | $2,500–$25,000/project |

**The tailwind:** SB 1036 (Sep 2026) increases compliance pressure on solar companies. Homeowners with malfunctioning systems from non-compliant installers will need independent repair specialists. This company is positioned to capture that demand.

## Competitive Advantage

1. **Repair specialist** — not an installer. Homeowners trust a specialist over a company trying to upsell a new install.
2. **Hert Renewables partnership** — immediate commercial revenue from day one (no cold start).
3. **SB 1036 compliance ready** — properly licensed and insured when competitors scramble.
4. **Multi-trade coverage** — roofing + electrical + structural, unlike single-trade competitors.
5. **Smith Capital backing** — David's network and capital access provides stability.
