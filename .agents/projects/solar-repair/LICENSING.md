## Texas Licensing Requirements for Solar Repair

### TDLR — Solar Contractor Registration (SB 1036)
- **Effective:** September 1, 2026
- **Required for:** All solar retailers and contractors operating in Texas
- **Managed by:** Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation (TDLR)
- **Key requirement:** Must carry GL insurance before registration
- **Register at:** tdlr.texas.gov

### Electrical Contractor License (TECL)
- Required for any electrical work including inverter replacement, wiring, panel tie-in
- Must have a licensed Master Electrician on staff or as qualifier
- **Apply at:** tdlr.texas.gov/electrical

### Roofing Contractor Registration
- Texas does not require a statewide roofing license, but some municipalities do
- Check Pflugerville, Austin, Round Rock local requirements
- Hail/storm repair work may require specific registration
