## Business Overview

A solar repair and maintenance company specializing in **residential solar systems** as the primary market, with **commercial repair contracts** through a partnership with **Hert Renewables**. The company services the Austin/Pflugerville/Central Texas corridor, positioned as a specialist repair brand — not an installer — in a market where most players focus on new installation.

**The niche advantage:** Most solar companies are install-focused. As the Texas solar market matures (1M+ residential systems installed), repair and maintenance demand is growing rapidly. This company captures that underserved aftermarket.

## Market Context (Texas)

- Texas is the #2 solar state in the US — over 1.5M residential systems installed
- SB 1036 (effective September 1, 2026) requires all solar retailers/contractors to carry GL insurance — increases demand for compliant repair specialists
- Hail, wind, and extreme heat create consistent repair demand in Central Texas
- Most big installers (Tesla, SunPower, ADT Solar) have limited local repair capacity
- Repair market is fragmented — significant opportunity for a specialist brand
