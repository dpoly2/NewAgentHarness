## Company Overview

**Clarity Solar Services** is a solar repair and maintenance company based in Pflugerville, TX. We specialize in residential solar system repair as our primary market, with commercial repair work handled through a partnership with Hert Renewables.

**The positioning:** We are NOT an installer. We are the repair specialist — the company homeowners call when their solar system stops performing. Trust, clarity, and expertise are everything in this space.

## Visual Direction

### Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Sky Blue** (primary) | `#3A9BD5` | Logo, headers, CTA buttons |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | Backgrounds, negative space |
| **Slate Gray** | `#4A5568` | Body text, secondary elements |
| **Warm Gold** (accent) | `#F6C244` | Sun element in logo, highlights |

> The blue + white + gray combination reads clean, professional, and trustworthy — it distances the brand from the aggressive green/yellow solar installer aesthetic. The gold accent adds warmth and the solar sun connection without being cliché.

### Typography Direction

| Use | Style | Examples |
|-----|-------|---------|
| Logo wordmark | Geometric sans-serif, medium weight | Montserrat, Futura, Raleway |
| Headlines | Clean sans-serif, bold | Same family as wordmark |
| Body text | Readable sans-serif | Inter, Open Sans |

> Avoid script/cursive fonts. Avoid slab serifs. Keep it modern and clean.

## Logo Concepts (Design Direction)

### Concept A — "Sun + Signal" Mark
- Wordmark: "CLARITY" in bold geometric caps, "SOLAR SERVICES" in lighter weight below
- Icon: Stylized sun with clean, minimal rays — one ray subtly shaped as a wifi/signal arc suggesting monitoring and performance
- Color: Sky blue wordmark, gold sun icon
- Feel: Technical but approachable

### Concept B — "Clear Ray" Mark
- Wordmark: "Clarity Solar" in medium weight, "Services" smaller below
- Icon: A single diagonal ray of light cutting through a simple cloud shape — represents "clearing" problems
- Color: Blue gradient ray, white cloud, slate gray wordmark
- Feel: Clean, modern, story-driven

### Concept C — "Rooftop Clarity" Mark
- Wordmark: Full name horizontal
- Icon: Simplified roofline silhouette with a solar panel and a small sunburst above — all in clean flat design
- Color: Sky blue + slate gray
- Feel: Instantly communicates "solar on homes" — most literal, most practical for local SEO

> **Recommended starting point:** Concept A or C. Concept A has the most scalability across digital and vehicle wrap. Concept C is strongest for local search and homeowner recognition.
