**Last Updated:** 2026-05-22

---

## Target Entity Structure

```
DAVID SMITH (Individual / Manager)
│
├── Smith Capital Properties LLC (Parent Holding Company)
│   ├── YEPC Development LLC ──────────── YEPC CR 132 project
│   ├── The Elevation LLC ──────────────── ATX private experience series
│   ├── Nutrue Apparel LLC ─────────────── apparel brand
│   └── [Future property LLCs] ─────────── 1 LLC per acquisition
│
└── Xtreme Force Track Club 501(c)(3) ─── nonprofit anchor (separate, no ownership)
    └── Programmatic partnership with YEPC Dev LLC via Management Agreement
```

## Rationale
- **Liability isolation:** Each project in its own LLC protects Smith Capital from cross-liability
- **Grant/loan layering:** XFTC 501(c)(3) unlocks nonprofit grant eligibility; Smith Capital for-profit entities access SBA/CDFI debt
- **Tax efficiency:** Pass-through taxation via LLC structure to David personally
- **Investor readiness:** Clean entity structure makes it easy to bring in partners per project

## Texas LLC Formation Steps (per entity)
1. File Certificate of Formation — sos.state.tx.us ($300)
2. Obtain EIN — IRS.gov (free, immediate)
3. Draft Operating Agreement (can be single-member)
4. Open dedicated business bank account (Frost, Chase Business, or Mercury)
5. Register for Texas franchise tax (if revenue > $2.47M threshold — most LLCs are exempt early years)

---

## Operating Agreement Provisions (Standard)
- Single-member: David Smith as sole member and manager
- Succession clause: designate a successor manager
- Purpose clause: specific to each entity's business
- Dissolution clause: required for nonprofit-adjacent grants
- Capital contribution schedule
