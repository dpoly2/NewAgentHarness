# Smith Capital Properties LLC — Reactivation Checklist

## The Problem
Annual Franchise Tax Reports and/or Public Information Reports (PIR) were not filed
with the Texas Comptroller. Texas LLCs do NOT have quarterly returns — only annual
filings due May 15 each year. Missing those triggers forfeiture and "Inactive" status.

---

## Reactivation Steps

### ☐ Step 1 — Confirm Status & Amounts Owed
**Deadline:** Do this TODAY
**URL:** https://mycpa.cpa.state.tx.us/coa/
- Search: "Smith Capital Properties"
- Note: status, years missed, and any balance owed
- Screenshot the result for your records

---

### ☐ Step 2 — File All Past-Due Reports
**Deadline:** Within 3 days of Step 1
**URL:** https://www.comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/franchise/
- Log into Texas Comptroller WebFile
- File every missed **Franchise Tax Report** (due May 15 each year)
- File every missed **Public Information Report (PIR)**
- Most small LLCs with <$2.47M revenue file **"No Tax Due"** — but the filing is still required
- Save confirmation numbers for each filing

---

### ☐ Step 3 — Pay Any Penalties
**Deadline:** Same session as Step 2
- Penalty: 5% of tax due per month (minimum $50 if tax was owed)
- If "No Tax Due" — penalties may be minimal or waivable
- Pay online via WebFile — get receipt/confirmation

---

### ☐ Step 4 — File Certificate of Reinstatement with Texas SOS
**Deadline:** Within 2 business days of Comptroller clearance
**URL:** https://www.sos.state.tx.us/corp/forms_option.shtml
- Form: **Form 811 — Certificate of Reinstatement**
- Filing fee: **$75**
- Requires Comptroller tax clearance letter/confirmation
- Can file online via SOSDirect: https://www.sos.state.tx.us/corp/sosda/index.shtml

---

### ☐ Step 5 — Obtain Certificate of Good Standing
**Deadline:** 3–5 business days after SOS approval
**URL:** https://www.sos.state.tx.us/corp/certorders.shtml
- Order a **Certificate of Good Standing** (also called Certificate of Existence)
- Fee: ~$15 online
- Required for: bank accounts, contracts, financing, YEPC land deals
