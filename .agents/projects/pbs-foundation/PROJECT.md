# Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity — Collegiate Travel Foundation
## Master Project File

---

## Project Overview
- **Project Name:** Phi Beta Sigma Collegiate Travel & Leadership Foundation (working title)
- **Parent Organization:** Psi Beta Sigma 1914 (existing nonprofit)
- **Beneficiaries:** Collegiate members of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity
- **Purpose:** Offset conference travel costs and support educational/leadership development for collegiate members
- **Status:** Pre-Formation / Planning Phase
- **Entity Type Target:** 501(c)(3) Public Charity

---

## Mission Statement (Draft)
"To support the educational and leadership development of collegiate members of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity by providing financial assistance for conference participation, travel, and access to transformative leadership opportunities."

## Program Design — Conference Travel Grants

### Eligibility (Draft)
- Active collegiate member in good standing with Phi Beta Sigma chapter
- Enrolled full-time at accredited college or university
- Demonstrates financial need (FAFSA or income documentation)
- Has not previously received grant (or limit 1x per year)
- Submits application by deadline

### Award Structure (Draft)
- Tier 1 — Regional Conference: Up to $300
- Tier 2 — State Conference: Up to $500
- Tier 3 — National Convention: Up to $1,000

### Application Timeline (Draft)
- Applications open 8 weeks before each conference
- Awards announced 4 weeks before conference
- Recipients submit post-conference impact report within 30 days
