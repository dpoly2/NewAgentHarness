# Foundation Charter: Elevate Scholars Foundation
**Status:** Draft
**Date:** 2026-05-19
**Adopted:** Pending Board Formation

---

## Article I: Name

The name of this foundation shall be the **Elevate Scholars Foundation**, hereinafter referred to as the "Foundation".

---

## Article II: Mission

The mission of the Foundation is to support the educational and leadership development of collegiate members of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity by providing financial assistance for conference participation.

---

## Article III: Board of Directors

### Section 1: Composition
The Foundation shall be governed by a Board of Directors consisting of no fewer than five (5) and no more than eleven (11) members.

### Section 2: Responsibilities
The Board of Directors shall be responsible for overseeing the activities and operations of the Foundation, including but not limited to fundraising, financial management, program development, and strategic planning.

---

## Article IV: Officers

### Section 1: Titles
The officers of the Foundation shall consist of a President, Vice President, Secretary, and Treasurer.

### Section 2: Responsibilities

- **President** — shall preside over meetings of the Board of Directors and provide leadership and direction to the Foundation.
- **Vice President** — shall assist the President and perform the duties of the President in their absence.
- **Secretary** — shall be responsible for maintaining accurate records of all meetings and correspondence of the Foundation.
- **Treasurer** — shall oversee the financial affairs of the Foundation, including budgeting, accounting, and financial reporting.

---

## Article V: Funding

The Foundation shall raise funds through donations, grants, fundraising events, and other fundraising activities. All funds raised shall be used exclusively to support the mission and programs of the Foundation.

---

## Article VI: Programs

The Foundation shall establish programs and initiatives aimed at providing financial assistance to collegiate members of Phi Beta Sigma Fraternity for conference participation. These programs may include scholarships, travel grants, and other forms of assistance.

---

## Article VII: Governance

The Foundation shall operate in accordance with its bylaws, which shall be adopted and amended as necessary by a majority vote of the Board of Directors.

---

## Article VIII: Dissolution

In the event of the dissolution of the Foundation, any remaining assets shall be distributed to one or more organizations with similar purposes, as determined by the Board of Directors.

---

## Article IX: Amendments

This charter may be amended by a two-thirds (2/3) vote of the Board of Directors, provided that notice of the proposed amendment has been given to all members of the Board at least thirty (30) days prior to the vote.

---

## Article X: Effective Date

This charter shall become effective upon adoption by the Board of Directors.
