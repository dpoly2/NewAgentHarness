# Project Night King — Master Build Sheet
**Project Number:** 17
**Status:** Active — Phase I Build Planning
**Owner:** David Smith
**Class:** 2021 Jeep Gladiator Mojave

---

## Concept
A blacked-out, Wakanda-inspired Gladiator build designed for SEMA consideration and social media reveal.
Two modes: **Shadow Mode** (stealth daily) and **War Mode** (full aggressive stance + lighting activated).

---

## Phase I — Locked Aggressive Spec

### Suspension & Lift
- **Lift:** TeraFlex 3" Lift Kit (coil spacer + rear AAL)
- **UCAs:** TeraFlex HD Upper Control Arms
- **Target stance:** +3" front / +2.5" rear for slight rake

### Wheels & Tires
- **Wheels:** Fuel Maverick D538 — Matte Black w/ Gloss Black ring — 20x10 (-18 offset)
- **Tires:** Nitto Ridge Grappler — 37x12.50R20
- **Spare:** Matching Fuel Maverick + Nitto Ridge Grappler (rear carrier)

### Exterior
- **Fenders:** Bushwacker Pocket Style Fender Flares (black textured)
- **Bumpers:** Rough Country Front Stubby + Rear Step Bumper delete
- **Grille:** Aftermarket mesh grille — blacked out
- **Hood:** Vented hood scoop — matte black
- **Mirrors:** Blacked-out OEM mirror caps
- **Bed:** Tonneau cover (retractable, flush) + bed lighting strip
- **Rock Sliders:** Smittybilt XRC Rock Rails

### Lighting
- **Underglow:** Oracle Multicolor LED Kit (activated in War Mode — blue/purple Wakandan palette)
- **Light Bar:** Rigid Industries 40" SR-Series (roof mount)
- **Rock Lights:** Oracle Rock Light Kit (8-piece, RGB)
- **Headlights:** Blacked-out housing + LED retrofit

### Interior
- **Seats:** Corbeau Sport Seats (black/gunmetal)
- **Steering Wheel:** Aftermarket flat-bottom — carbon fiber/suede
- **Dash:** Custom carbon fiber wrap
- **Audio:** Alpine system upgrade

### Paint / Wrap
- **Base:** Satin Black full wrap (3M 1080 Satin Black)
- **Accents:** Midnight Purple ghost stripe on hood + rear fender
- **Badging:** All badges blacked out / removed
