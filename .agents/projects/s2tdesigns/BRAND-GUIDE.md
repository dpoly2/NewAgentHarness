## Brand Identity Overview
S2T Designs positions itself as a **trusted, results-driven design partner** for small businesses, nonprofits, and community organizations. The brand should feel professional and creative — approachable but polished.

---

## Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Confident, clear, approachable — no jargon
- **Personality:** Expert friend who explains things plainly
- **Avoid:** Corporate stiffness, buzzword-heavy copy, over-promising
- **Example:** "We build sites that work hard so you don't have to." ✅
- **Not:** "We leverage cutting-edge digital ecosystems to synergize your online presence." ❌
