# LEBC — Sample Social Media Campaign
**Client:** Little Ebenezer Baptist Church
**Prepared By:** S2T Designs / social-copywriter + social-content-strategist

**Platforms:** Facebook (primary) · Instagram (rebuild) · Google Business

## CAMPAIGN THEME: "119 Years Strong. Come Home."

June is the launch month. The strategy: establish LEBC's presence as the community's
spiritual home in Hutto — warm, multigenerational, and deeply rooted.

Every piece of content reinforces one of 4 pillars:
🙏 Faith & Inspiration | 🏛️ Church Life | 🤝 Community | 📢 Announcements
