## RECOMMENDATIONS

### Immediate Wins (Week 1–2)

1. **Instagram: Rebuild the profile**
   - Consider handle change to @lebchuttotx or @lebchurch or @littleebenezerhutto
   - Rewrite bio: "Little Ebenezer Baptist Church | Hutto, TX | Sundays 11AM | 119 years of faith, community & love 🙏 | Link below 👇"
   - Add website link
   - Update profile photo to church logo or exterior photo
   - Post 3 Reels immediately (Sunday worship clip, welcome from Pastor Spence, church history)

2. **Facebook: Optimize the page**
   - Pin a welcome post with service times and website link
   - Create a Facebook Event for every upcoming special event
   - Enable Facebook Live for Sunday worship (even phone quality is fine)
   - Respond to all 9 reviews with a thank-you from Pastor Spence
   - Add a "Contact Us" call-to-action button

3. **Google Business Profile: Create immediately**
   - LEBC does not appear to have a Google Business listing — this is a critical miss
   - A local church without Google Maps presence loses visitors who search "Baptist church Hutto TX"
   - Set up: name, address, hours, photos, service times

### Content Strategy (Month 1)

**Content Pillars (4 core topics):**
1. 🙏 **Faith & Inspiration** — Scripture, devotionals, sermon highlights
2. 🏛️ **Church Life** — Service moments, ministry updates, behind-the-scenes
3. 🤝 **Community** — Outreach events, local partnerships, congregation spotlights
4. 📢 **Announcements** — Events, special services, upcoming programs

**Posting Schedule (Starter — Tier 1):**
| Platform | Frequency | Best Times |
|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Facebook | 4x/week | Sun 12pm, Tue 7pm, Thu 7pm, Sat 10am |
| Instagram | 3x/week | Sun 1pm, Wed 7pm, Fri 6pm |
| Facebook Live | 1x/week | Sunday worship (11am) |
| Instagram Reels | 2x/month | Sunday sermon clip, mid-week devotional |

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Faith & Inspiration (broadest appeal, highest share rate)
- 30% Church Life (builds community loyalty)
- 20% Announcements (drives event attendance)
- 10% Community/Outreach (shows LEBC's heart for Hutto)
