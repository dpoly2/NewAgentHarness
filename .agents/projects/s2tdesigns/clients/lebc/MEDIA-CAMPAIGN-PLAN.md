## PART 2 — SERMON PODCAST

### "The Word from Little Ebenezer"

**Concept:** Weekly sermon audio podcast hosted by Pastor Arthur L. Spence. Pull directly from Sunday YouTube livestreams — no extra recording required.

**Format:**
- Length: 30–50 minutes (full sermon)
- Drops: Every Sunday by 3PM (auto-published after service)
- Host: Pastor Arthur L. Spence
- Intro/Outro: 30-second branded audio bumper

**Episode Structure:**
1. Branded intro music (10 sec)
2. "Welcome to The Word from Little Ebenezer — I'm Pastor Arthur Spence" (20 sec)
3. Full sermon audio (30–50 min)
4. Outro: "Join us in person at 215 S. Brushy St, Hutto TX. Find us on YouTube, Facebook, and everywhere you listen to podcasts." (20 sec)

**Distribution (All Free):**
| Platform | Audience |
|----------|---------|
| Spotify for Podcasters | Spotify + Samsung + Alexa |
| Apple Podcasts | iPhone users |
| Google Podcasts / YouTube Music | Android users |
| Amazon Music | Echo device users |
| Pocket Casts | Podcast power users |

**Production Workflow (S2T Handles):**
1. LEBC uploads Sunday service to YouTube (they already do this)
2. S2T downloads audio, cleans it up, adds intro/outro
3. Episode published to all platforms by Sunday 3PM
4. Clip pulled for Instagram Reels + Facebook post

**Why This Works:**
- Zero extra work for the church — they already record everything
- Members can listen during commutes, at the gym, doing chores
- Reaches people who will never see a Facebook post
- Every episode is evergreen content — searchable forever on Spotify/Apple

## PART 3 — YOUTH PODCAST NETWORK

### "LEBC Youth Media" — Three-Show Umbrella

This is the growth engine. Three age-specific shows under one brand — each hosted by LEBC members themselves.

---

### SHOW 1 — "Little Lights"
**Ages: 4–12 | Parents listen with kids**

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Format | 8–12 minutes |
| Host | Youth ministry leader + 1–2 kid co-hosts |
| Drop Day | Fridays (weekend car ride timing) |
| Tone | Warm, playful, storytelling-driven |
| Topics | Bible stories retold for kids, "what would Jesus do" scenarios, kids answering big faith questions |
| Why It Works | Parents actively search for faith-based kids content — massive underserved niche on Spotify Kids |

**Sample Episodes:**
- "Why Do We Go to Church? (We Asked the Kids)"
- "The Story of David and Goliath — But Make It Hutto"
- "What Is Prayer? 6-Year-Olds Explain"
- "Why Should I Be Kind When It's Hard?"

---

### SHOW 2 — "The Upper Room"
**Ages: 13–18 | Teen-hosted, teen-focused**

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Format | 15–20 minutes |
| Host | 2–3 LEBC teens (rotating panel) |
| Drop Day | Wednesdays (midweek faith boost) |
| Tone | Real, honest, peer-to-peer — NOT preachy |
| Topics | Faith vs. school culture, identity, peer pressure, college decisions, mental health, social media |
| Why It Works | Teens share content made BY other teens. This is the viral show. One good episode spreads through a whole high school. |

**Sample Episodes:**
- "Is It Okay to Doubt Your Faith?"
- "How Do You Stay Christian at a Public School?"
- "Dating, Boundaries, and What the Bible Actually Says"
- "We Asked Our Parents About Growing Up in the Church"
- "College Prep + Faith — Can You Have Both?"

**Growth Hack:** Feature Pflugerville and Hutto High School students. Local name recognition = local shares = new visitors.

---

### SHOW 3 — "Built Different"
**Ages: 19–25 | Young adult conversations**

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Format | 20–30 minutes |
| Host | 1–2 LEBC young adults (consistent hosts) |
| Drop Day | Thursdays |
| Tone | Mature, real, aspirational — faith meets real life |
| Topics | Career + calling, relationships, adulting with faith, community service, mental health, finances |
| Why It Works | Young adults (19–25) are the most likely to leave the church — this gives them a reason to stay connected digitally even when life gets busy |

**Sample Episodes:**
- "How Do You Keep Your Faith When Life Gets Hard?"
- "Is It Okay to Not Have It All Figured Out at 22?"
- "Serving Your Community — Why LEBC Shows Up for Hutto"
- "The Real Talk on Relationships and Faith"
- "We're Young, Black, and Christian in Texas — Here's What That Means"

**Guest Opportunity:** Invite local professionals, fraternity/sorority members (BGLO connection), educators, and business owners as guests. Builds LEBC's community network fast.
