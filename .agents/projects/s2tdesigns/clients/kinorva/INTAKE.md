## Current Brand Analysis (from Attached One-Pager)

### Existing Logo
- **Mark:** A stylized V-shape / fork-like monogram (resembles a tuning fork or abstract Wi-Fi signal inverted)
- **Wordmark:** "KINORVA" in a clean, wide-tracked all-caps sans-serif
- **Tagline:** "SMART COOLER SOLUTIONS" in a smaller tracked sans-serif below
- **Colors used on one-pager:** Dark navy/charcoal `#1B2A3B` background sections, teal/green accent `#4CAF8E`, white text, gold/orange accent bars for pricing callouts

### What's Working
- The V/fork mark is distinctive and memorable
- All-caps wordmark has a tech/industrial feel appropriate for the B2B warehouse space
- Color scheme is clean and professional

### What Could Be Stronger
- The mark feels generic — could belong to any tech company
- No connection between the mark and the "smart cooler / food / AI" concept
- Typography hierarchy on collateral could be tighter
- No brand system visible — needs defined color palette, type system, and usage rules
