## 10 Discovery Questions

| # | Question | Why It Matters |
|---|----------|---------------|
| 1 | What's driving the redesign — is it a rebrand, a refresh, or fixing something specific that bothers you? | Defines scope and urgency |
| 2 | Do you want to keep the V-shape mark or are you open to a completely new direction? | Determines concept approach |
| 3 | How should the new brand feel — more tech-forward, more approachable, or same energy just cleaner? | Drives visual direction |
| 4 | Any brands (in or out of your industry) whose visual identity you respect? | Style reference |
| 5 | Are you locked into the navy/teal colors, or is the palette open for evolution? | Color system scope |
| 6 | Beyond the logo, do you need collateral redesigned — the one-pager, pitch deck, sales sheets? | Scope the full project |
| 7 | Do you have a website that needs to match the new brand? | Web scope |
| 8 | Who is the decision maker for approvals — you (Hosie) or someone above you? | Speeds up revision process |
| 9 | What's your target timeline — do you have a pitch, event, or launch driving the deadline? | Sets the schedule |
| 10 | What's the budget you're working with for this project? | Sizes the proposal |

## Packages to Propose

### Option A — Logo Redesign Only [$750–$1,000]
- 3 logo concepts (mark + wordmark + tagline)
- 2 revision rounds
- Final files: SVG, AI, PNG (transparent, white, dark), PDF
- Timeline: 2 weeks

### Option B — Logo + Brand System [$1,400–$1,800] ⭐ RECOMMENDED
- Everything in Option A, plus:
- Formalized color palette (HEX, RGB, CMYK, PMS)
- Typography system (3 fonts, usage rules)
- Full brand guide PDF (10–15 pages)
- Logo usage rules + clear space guide
- Timeline: 3–4 weeks

### Option C — Full Brand + Collateral [$2,500–$3,500]
- Everything in Option B, plus:
- One-pager redesign (matching new brand)
- Pitch deck template (Google Slides or PowerPoint, 10–15 slides)
- Email signature template
- LinkedIn banner
- Timeline: 5–6 weeks

## Positioning for the Call

**Open with this:**
> "I reviewed your one-pager before this call — your messaging is already strong. The 'Your Biggest P&L Leak Has No Line Item' hook is excellent. What we're doing with the brand is making sure the visual identity matches the confidence of that copy. Right now the logo is serviceable — the redesign will make it undeniable."

**This does three things:**
1. Shows you actually know their business (not a generic pitch)
2. Compliments their existing work (builds rapport)
3. Frames the redesign as an upgrade, not a criticism
