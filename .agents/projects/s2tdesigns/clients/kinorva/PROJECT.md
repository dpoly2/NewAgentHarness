## Client Overview

Kinorva is a B2B technology company deploying AI-enabled smart coolers in warehouse and industrial facilities. Their value proposition is zero-cost deployment — no CapEx, no setup fees, no monthly fees — with AI-triggered restocking. They serve warehouse operators and facility managers looking to eliminate off-site food runs and the productivity loss they cause.

**Key stats from their collateral:**
- Eliminates $48K–$72K/year in untracked P&L loss per facility
- 68% of warehouse facilities have zero on-site food access
- Deployment: 24in W x 30in D unit, standard 110V outlet, 150 SKU capacity
- AI-triggered restocking, 24/7 availability, 48hr from implementation to fully stocked

**Business model:** Full turnkey — Kinorva absorbs all CapEx, inventory, and operational liabilities.
