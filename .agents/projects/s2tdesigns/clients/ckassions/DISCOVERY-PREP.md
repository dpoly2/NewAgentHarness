---

## Goal of This Call

Get enough information to:
1. Confirm the full scope (logo only vs. full brand package vs. e-commerce too)
2. Understand the brand personality and target customer
3. Get a budget range and timeline
4. Set expectations for deliverables and process
5. Close the engagement and collect a deposit

---

## 10 Discovery Questions to Ask

| # | Question | Why It Matters |
|---|----------|---------------|
| 1 | What does "CKassions" mean or stand for? | Informs the logo mark and brand story |
| 2 | Who is your target customer — age, lifestyle, vibe? | Drives design direction completely |
| 3 | What's the brand personality? (e.g. streetwear, luxury, athletic, faith-inspired) | Determines typography, color, tone |
| 4 | What apparel items are your priority — tees, hoodies, hats? | Scopes apparel phase deliverables |
| 5 | Do you have any existing logo or visual identity we're building from? | Determines starting point |
| 6 | Any brands, logos, or designers you love the look of? | Style reference for design direction |
| 7 | Where do you plan to sell the apparel — online, pop-ups, boutiques, all three? | Determines platform needs |
| 8 | What's your price point — budget, mid-range, premium, or luxury? | Affects brand positioning and design feel |
| 9 | What's your target launch date? | Sets the project timeline |
| 10 | What's your budget for the brand and design work? | Sizes the proposal correctly |

## Scope Packages to Propose

### Option A — Brand Essentials [$600–$900]
- Primary logo + alternate mark
- Color palette (5 colors with HEX/CMYK)
- Typography system (2 fonts)
- 1-page brand reference sheet (PDF)
- Files: SVG, PNG, PDF

### Option B — Full Brand Package [$1,200–$1,800]
Everything in Option A, plus:
- Full brand guide document (8–12 pages PDF)
- Social media kit (profile image, cover photo, 3 post templates)
- Apparel mockups (3 designs — chest logo, center graphic, back design)
- Print-ready files (300 DPI PNG + vector)

### Option C — Brand + Launch [$2,500–$3,500]
Everything in Option B, plus:
- E-commerce storefront setup (Shopify + Printful/Printify)
- 10 product listings (mockup to live product)
- Domain + hosting setup
- Social media account setup (Instagram, TikTok profile)
- 30-day post-launch support

## Process Overview to Share with Client

```
WEEK 1 — Discovery + Mood Board
  → Discovery call + intake form
  → Mood board (3 style directions) for approval

WEEK 2 — Logo Design
  → 3 logo concepts presented
  → Client picks direction + feedback
  → Revision round

WEEK 3 — Brand System
  → Color palette, typography, brand guide
  → Apparel design concepts

WEEK 4 — Delivery
  → Final brand guide PDF
  → Print-ready apparel files
  → Social media kit
  → Handoff call

[OPTIONAL PHASE 2 — E-commerce/Storefront: 2–3 additional weeks]
```
