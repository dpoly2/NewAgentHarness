## Overview
S2T Designs is a full-service web design and graphic design agency. The agency delivers custom websites, brand identities, print/digital graphics, and multi-platform web development for small businesses, nonprofits, and community organizations — with a focus on the Austin/Central Texas market and the BGLO/minority business community.

## Agency Philosophy
1. **Platform-first assessment** — Choose the best tool for the client's actual needs, not habit
2. **Performance-driven** — Every site must be fast, accessible, and mobile-first
3. **Ownership matters** — Educate clients on what they own and how to manage it
4. **Design with purpose** — Every visual decision should serve the business goal
