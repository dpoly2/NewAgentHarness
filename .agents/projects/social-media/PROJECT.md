## Overview
The S2T Designs Social Media Division handles full-service social media marketing for S2T clients and internal brand accounts. Services range from organic content strategy and campaign management to paid ad campaigns and community management.

## Campaign Workflow

```
1. CLIENT ONBOARDING
   └── Intake form → brand voice guide → platform audit → KPI setting

2. STRATEGY
   social-content-strategist → content pillars + monthly calendar

3. PRODUCTION
   social-copywriter → captions + scripts
   social-designer → graphics + video covers

4. REVIEW & APPROVAL
   social-project-lead reviews → client approves (or David approves for internal)

5. PUBLISH & ENGAGE
   Posts go live per schedule
   social-community-manager handles responses daily

6. PAID ADS (if applicable)
   social-ads-manager builds + launches campaigns
   social-analyst tracks spend + performance

7. REPORTING
   social-analyst → monthly report → social-project-lead → delivered to client
```
