## Priority Funding Targets

### 🏦 SBA Loans

| Program | Amount | Notes | Status |
|---------|--------|-------|--------|
| SBA 7(a) | Up to $5M | Best for acquisition + working capital. Requires 10–20% down. Use for building purchase or leasehold improvement. | Research |
| SBA 504 | Up to $5.5M | Fixed-rate, long-term for commercial real estate. Pairs with conventional lender. | Research |
| SBA Microloan | Up to $50K | Smaller working capital needs | Low priority |

**SBA Resources:**
- Austin SBDC: https://www.austinsbdc.org
- North Texas SBDC: https://ntsbdc.org
- Apply via SBA.gov — find approved lenders in Travis/Williamson County
