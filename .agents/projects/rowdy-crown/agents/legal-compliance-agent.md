## TABC Licensing Strategy

### License Type Needed
**Mixed Beverage Permit (MB)** — allows sale of all alcoholic beverages by the drink
Paired with: **Food and Beverage Certificate (FB)** if food revenue < 51% of gross

### TABC Timeline (Realistic)
| Step | Timeline |
|------|----------|
| Pre-application site check | 1–2 weeks |
| Application submission | Week 3 |
| Public notice posting (required 60 days) | Days 1–60 |
| TABC investigation | 60–90 days |
| License issued (if no objections) | 90–120 days total |

**Start TABC application as early as possible — it is the longest lead item.**

### TABC Key Requirements
- Applicant must be 21+, Texas resident or registered TX entity
- Building must be at least 300 ft from a church or school (measure from property line)
- No felony convictions for principals
- Must pass background check
- Location must be zoned for on-premise alcohol consumption

### Prior Permit Research

- Contact TABC directly: https://www.tabc.texas.gov | (512) 206-3333
