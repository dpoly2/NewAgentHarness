## Step-by-Step Formation

### Step 1 — Check Name Availability (5 min, free)
1. Go to: https://mycpa.cpa.state.tx.us/coa/Index.html (TX SOS Name Search)
2. Search "The Elevation LLC" — confirm it's available
3. Also check: "Elevation ATX LLC", "The Elevation ATX LLC" as backups
4. ✅ Name confirmed available → proceed to Step 2

---

### Step 2 — File Certificate of Formation (Form 205)
**Online filing (recommended — same-day processing):**
1. Go to: https://www.sos.state.tx.us/corp/sosda/index.shtml
2. Log in or create a SOS Direct account
3. Select: **Domestic Limited Liability Company (Form 205)**
4. Fill in:
   - **Entity name:** The Elevation LLC
   - **Registered agent:** David Smith (you can serve as your own RA)
   - **Registered office:** Your TX address (Smith Capital Properties address works)
   - **Organizer:** David Smith
   - **Purpose:** General — entertainment venue and hospitality services
5. **Fee:** $300 (credit/debit card)
6. Submit → Certificate issued within 24 hours

**Mail alternative (slower — 5–7 days):**
- Download Form 205 from: https://www.sos.state.tx.us/corp/forms_boc.shtml
- Mail + $300 check to TX SOS
- Not recommended — wastes time

---

### Step 3 — Get EIN from IRS (15 min, free, same-day)
1. Go to: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
2. Select: **LLC**
3. Answer questions — purpose: "Started a new business"
4. **Download and save the EIN confirmation letter** — you will need this for every loan and permit application

---

### Step 4 — Draft Operating Agreement
**You need this before opening a bank account or accepting investors.**

Key provisions to include:
- **Manager-managed** — David Smith as sole managing member
- **Membership:** David Smith — 80% initial interest
- **Reserved equity:** 20% authorized for future angel investor round
- **Distributions:** Quarterly, after debt service + 3-month cash reserve
- **Buy-sell:** Right of first refusal on any member's interest
- **Non-compete:** 3-year, 50-mile radius for departing members with >5% stake
- **Smith Capital lease:** Operating agreement authorizes sublease from Smith Capital Properties

Options:
- **DIY:** Use Rocket Lawyer or LegalZoom ($99–$149) for a basic Texas LLC operating agreement
- **Attorney:** $500–$1,500 — recommended once you bring in investors
- **Interim:** Use a free template now, have attorney clean it up before the angel round

---

### Step 5 — Open Business Bank Account
**Do this the day you get your EIN.**

What you need:
- The Elevation LLC Certificate of Formation
- EIN confirmation letter
- Operating Agreement
- Government-issued ID (David Smith)
- Opening deposit (varies by bank — $25–$500)

**Recommended banks for Black-owned businesses:**
| Bank | Notes |
|------|-------|
| **Greenwood** | Black-owned fintech bank — culturally aligned |
| **Lili / Mercury** | Business-focused, fast setup, no minimums |
| **Chase Business** | Largest network, required for some SBA lenders |
| **Frost Bank** | Strong Texas community bank — good for SBA relationship |

**Recommendation:** Open at **Chase** (SBA preferred lender) AND **Greenwood** — use Chase for loan transactions, Greenwood for operations.

---

### Step 6 — Texas Sales Tax Permit (free)
1. Go to: https://comptroller.texas.gov/taxes/permit/
2. Register for Texas Sales and Use Tax Permit
3. Required before making any taxable sales (drinks, merchandise)
4. Takes 2–4 weeks to receive

---

### Step 7 — Texas Employer Registration (before first hire)
1. Go to: https://www.twc.texas.gov/businesses/register-your-business
2. Register with Texas Workforce Commission
3. Required before hiring any W-2 employees
4. Do this 2–3 months before your target hiring date
