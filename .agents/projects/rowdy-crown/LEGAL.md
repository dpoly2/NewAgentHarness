- **Late Hours:** Standard MB permits service until 2 AM. Late Hours Certificate extends to midnight+ (required for a nightclub).
- **Minors:** No one under 18 may be on premises during nightclub hours unless parent is present (check local rules — some areas differ).

| **Lease-to-own** | Best of both — lower entry, future buyout | Negotiation complexity |
