## Break-Even Analysis

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Monthly fixed costs (Year 1) | ~$109,481 |
| Average revenue per guest (blended) | ~$58 |
| Guests needed to break even (monthly) | ~1,887 guests/month |
| Operating nights per month | ~17 nights |
| Guests needed per night (break-even) | ~111 guests/night |

**Conclusion:** Break-even is achieved at just 111 guests per night — a very low bar for a venue with 400+ capacity. Even at 30% capacity, the venue covers fixed costs.
