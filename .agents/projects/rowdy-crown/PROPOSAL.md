## Executive Summary

The Elevation ATX is a proposed upscale nightclub and entertainment venue to be developed at 15119 N IH-35 Service Rd, Pflugerville, TX 78660 — the former Bombshells Restaurant & Bar location. The concept merges Western culture with Black excellence to create a one-of-a-kind entertainment destination serving the fastest-growing Black middle-class corridor in Central Texas.

Smith Capital Properties will acquire or control the real estate asset. A separate operating entity, The Elevation LLC, will manage day-to-day operations, events, and hospitality. The project requires an estimated $1.35M–$2.9M in total capitalization and projects $2.5M–$4.5M in annual gross revenue at stabilized operations.

There is currently **no comparable upscale Black-owned nightlife destination** in the Austin/Pflugerville/Round Rock MSA. Rowdy Crown fills that void with a premium, curated experience.

## The Opportunity

### Market Context
- Austin MSA Black population: ~100,000+, growing 8–12% annually
- Pflugerville/Wells Branch corridor: one of the fastest-growing Black middle-class suburbs in Texas
- Current nightlife gap: zero upscale, Black-owned full-service nightclub venues north of downtown Austin
- The former Bombshells location at 15119 N IH-35 is a purpose-built entertainment venue sitting vacant — fully equipped infrastructure at a fraction of new construction cost

### Why Now
- Post-pandemic nightlife pent-up demand remains high
- Austin's Black professional class is growing and underserved by existing nightlife
- IH-35 corridor development pressure means this site will not remain available indefinitely
- Brand "Rowdy Cowboy" cultural moment is at peak cultural resonance nationally — first mover advantage

## The Concept

**The Elevation ATX** — Where the Range Meets the Rooftop.

An upscale Western-themed nightclub and entertainment venue rooted in Black culture and excellence. Not a theme night. Not a one-off event. A **permanent institution** — a place Austin's Black professional community calls its own.

### Design Direction
- **Exterior:** Bold signage, rope-light marquee, valet parking lane
- **Main Floor:** Dark reclaimed wood, leather banquettes, brass fixtures, moody Edison lighting, DJ booth elevated center-rear
- **Bar:** Full-length custom bar — 40+ seats, backlit whiskey/bourbon wall, live edge countertops
- **Stage:** Live performance stage — flexible for band, DJ, comedy, spoken word
- **VIP Section:** Elevated platform, velvet rope, dedicated bottle service staff
- **Crown Suite:** Private event room — seats 40, AV system, private bar
- **Dress Code:** Enforced. Smart casual minimum. Western chic encouraged.

### Programming Calendar
| Night | Programming |
|-------|-------------|
| Thursday | Industry Night — discounted drinks for hospitality workers, R&B DJ |
| Friday | The Main Event — DJ, bottle service, dress code strictly enforced |
| Saturday | Crown Night — live act + DJ, VIP priority, highest cover |
| Sunday | Brunch + Bourbon — daytime to evening, live acoustic, brunch menu |
| Monthly | Brand activation nights, comedy shows, artist showcases |

## Site Due Diligence Priorities

1. **Building condition assessment** — structural, HVAC, plumbing, electrical
2. **TABC zoning eligibility** — confirm mixed beverage permit availability at this address
3. **Current ownership** — identify property owner, assess purchase vs. lease vs. lease-option
4. **ADA compliance** — assess gaps, estimate retrofit cost
5. **Parking capacity** — count spaces, assess valet feasibility
6. **Noise ordinance** — Pflugerville/Travis County boundary, outdoor sound limits
