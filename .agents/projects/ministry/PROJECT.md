## The Expository SoulSpeak Method

The foundation of all content produced by this team.

**The 6 P's:**
- **Presence** — Command the room before a word is spoken
- **Pace** — Control the speed and rhythm of delivery
- **Power** — Dynamic range from whisper to crescendo
- **Pause** — Strategic silence as a spiritual tool
- **Passion** — Authentic emotional movement
- **Precision** — Every word earns its place

**The 3-Stage Arc (Every Sermon):**
1. **Explain** — Root it in the text (exposition)
2. **Feel** — Bridge to human experience (testimony)
3. **Release** — Land it with power and celebration (declaration)

**The SoulSpeak Formula:**
> Teach it clearly. Feel it honestly. Deliver it musically. End it powerfully.

## Content Outputs

### Sermons
- Full manuscript with delivery markup
- Cadence markers, pause indicators, emotional arc guidance
- Memorizable opening and closing lines
- 10–40 minutes of delivery material

### Bible Study
- Weekly lessons (45–60 min facilitated)
- Discussion questions (observation → interpretation → application → testimony)
- Personal application challenge
- Compatible with Sunday sermon themes

### Sunday School
- 5 age tracks: Little Lights (4–8), Junior (9–12), Youth (13–18), Young Adult (19–25), Adult (26+)
- 4–8 week units per quarter
- Teacher notes + student activities + memory verses

### Delivery Training
- 30-Day SoulSpeak Training Program (30 min/day, 5 days/week)
- Weekly drills: control → musicality → emotional movement → live simulation
- Self-grading rubric and feedback framework
- Pre-sermon checklist

## Style Guide Reference
The full Expository SoulSpeak Delivery Training Plan is embedded in the `ministry-delivery-coach` agent. All agents reference this guide when producing content.

Key principles:
- Never start at level 10 — always build
- The greatest preachers build like a song
- Silence is often where the Spirit moves
- Do not rush the close
- Your body should underline the message, not compete with it
