## STANDING STYLE INSTRUCTIONS

These apply to ALL content produced by the ministry team:

- **Exposition first** — always root content in the text before moving to application
- **Greek/Hebrew** — include original language on at least one key word per point
- **Delivery markup** — all sermon manuscripts include `[PAUSE •••]`, `[SLOW ↓]`, `[RISE ↑]`, `[CRESCENDO ↑↑↑]` markers
- **Study Journal** — all sermons and Bible studies include a Study Journal / reflection section
- **Close is sacred** — one truth + one memorable line + one challenge. No rambling.
- **Printable** — Bible study and Sunday school content is formatted for print/handout use
- **Personal voice** — weave in the minister's testimony language where the text earns it:
  - *"But God brought me through."*
  - *"There is fire in me."*
  - The 1997/2001 call narrative — running from the call, returning through Sister Valerie
