## 🎤 PROMPT 1 — FULL SERMON MANUSCRIPT

> Build me a full sermon based on the theme: **[Insert Theme Here]**, using the scripture **[Insert Scripture Here]**.
>
> Please include:
> - A theologically rich introduction that sets up the theme
> - An expository 3-point outline with scripture integration
> - Expanded Expository Insight for each point (include Greek/Hebrew where relevant, plus theological and practical reflection)
> - Preacher's Training Notes throughout to help sharpen pulpit craft
> - A Preacher's Study Journal section with personal reflection prompts, cross-references, and prayer before preaching
> - Style must reflect Expository SoulSpeak: deep exposition (Dr. Marcus Cosby) + soulful illustrative delivery (Dr. Marvin Winans)
>
> Format the response as a full sermon manuscript, suitable for preaching, teaching, and future print or slide conversion.

**When to use:** Sunday message, revival message, special occasion sermon
**Output:** Full manuscript with delivery markup, study journal, memorizable open/close

## 📖 PROMPT 2 — BIBLE STUDY LESSON

## STANDING STYLE INSTRUCTIONS

These apply to ALL content produced by the ministry team:

- **Exposition first** — always root content in the text before application
- **Greek/Hebrew** — include original language on at least one key word per point
- **Delivery markup** — all sermon manuscripts include `[PAUSE •••]`, `[RISE ↑]`, `[CRESCENDO ↑↑↑]` markers
- **Study Journal** — all sermons include the Preacher's Study Journal section
- **Close is sacred** — one truth + one memorable line + one challenge. No rambling.
- **Personal voice** — weave in the minister's testimony language where natural:
  - *"But God brought me through."*
  - *"There is fire in me."*
  - The 1997/2001 call narrative (use sparingly — when the text earns it)
