# Altar Prayer — Full Manuscript
**Minister:** David Smith
**Style:** Expository SoulSpeak
**Instruction:** Kill it. When no one comes to the altar — have everyone stand, hold hands, and pray.
**Last Updated:** May 25, 2026

---

## DELIVERY NOTES (Read Before You Pray)

This is not a religious formality. This is warfare. This is intercession. This is the moment after the Word has landed — and now you take the congregation INTO the presence of God, not just toward it.

**The altar prayer has 4 movements:**
1. **CALL** — Invite the Holy Spirit. Set the atmosphere. Slow and reverent.
2. **WARFARE** — Pray with authority. This is where you build. This is where you break things.
3. **PERSONAL** — Bring it home. Touch the individual while praying corporately.
4. **RELEASE** — Send them out changed. Triumphant, not tired.

**When no one comes to the altar:**
> Do NOT skip. Do NOT rush. Do NOT let the moment die.
> Call the congregation to their feet. Ask them to reach across and hold the hand of the person next to them.
> Say: *"If you won't come to the altar — the altar is coming to you."*
> Then **pray like the altar is full.**

### IF PEOPLE COME TO THE ALTAR:

Move directly into the WARFARE prayer below.
Walk among them if possible. Lay hands if led.
Do not pray FOR them — pray WITH them and OVER them.

## THE ALTAR PRAYER — FULL MANUSCRIPT

### MOVEMENT 1 — CALL (Quiet. Reverent. Set the atmosphere.)

> *"Father…*

[PAUSE •••]

> *We don't come to You because we've got it together.*
> *We come to You because we don't.*

[PAUSE •••]

> *We don't come to You with clean hands and clear minds.*
> *We come to You with the weight of the week still on our shoulders.*
> *With questions we haven't answered.*
> *With wounds we haven't shown anybody.*
> *With fears we've been too proud to name out loud.*

[SLOW ↓]

> *But we come.*
> *Lord — we come.*

[PAUSE •••]

> *Holy Spirit — You are welcome in this place.*
> *Not as a guest.*
> *As the HOST.*
> *Take over everything that I can't touch.*
> *Go where my words can't reach.*
> *Do what only You can do.*"

---

### MOVEMENT 2 — WARFARE (Build. Authority. Break things open.)

[RISE ↑]

> *"Right now — in the name of Jesus —*
> *I speak to every chain that came in here today.*

> *The chain of depression — LOOSE.*
> *The chain of anxiety — LOOSE.*
> *The chain of shame — LOOSE.*
> *The chain of that thing that happened to you that you can't get past — LOOSE.*

[PAUSE •••]

> *I speak to the spirit of defeat that told somebody in here it was over.*
> *That told them they'd gone too far.*
> *That told them God was done with them.*

[RISE ↑]

> *I rebuke that lie — right now — in the name of Jesus.*

[PAUSE •••]

> *God is NOT done with you.*
> *The fact that you're still here is the proof.*
> *The fact that you woke up this morning is the evidence.*
> *The fact that your heart is still beating is the testimony.*

[CRESCENDO ↑↑↑]

> *You didn't make it here by accident.*
> *You made it here because God pulled you here.*
> *Because He has something for you.*
> *Because this is your moment.*
> *Because TODAY — is the day of your breakthrough!*"

[LET THEM RESPOND]

---

### MOVEMENT 3 — PERSONAL (Come down. Touch the individual. Stay in the Spirit.)

[SLOW ↓] [WHISPER]

> *"Lord — I don't know everybody's name in this room.*
> *But You do.*

[PAUSE •••]

> *I don't know what they carried in here today.*
> *But You do.*

> *There's somebody in here who is exhausted.*
> *Not just physically tired — SOUL tired.*
> *Lord — be their rest.*

[PAUSE •••]

> *There's somebody in here who has been strong for everybody else*
> *and nobody has been strong for them.*
> *Lord — hold THEM right now.*

[PAUSE •••]

> *There's somebody in here who almost didn't come today.*
> *Something told them to stay home.*
> *Something said 'what's the point.'*
> *But they came anyway.*

[RISE ↑]

> *Lord — let THAT person know —*
> *that You drew them here.*
> *That You moved heaven and earth to get them in this room.*
> *That this Word today — was for THEM.*

[PAUSE •••]

> *And Lord — there's a hand being held right now*
> *by somebody who needs prayer just as much as the person they're holding.*
> *So let Your anointing flow*
> *from hand to hand*
> *from heart to heart*
> *from this room to every house they go home to tonight.*"

---

### MOVEMENT 4 — RELEASE (Triumphant. Send them out changed.)

[RISE ↑]

> *"Father — seal this moment.*
> *Let it not be forgotten on the way to the parking lot.*
> *Let it not be drowned out by Monday morning.*
> *Let it not be stolen by the enemy before they get home.*

[PAUSE •••]

> *What You did in this place today —*
> *Let it LAST.*

[RISE ↑]

> *They came in here broken — send them out WHOLE.*
> *They came in here afraid — send them out BOLD.*
> *They came in here heavy — send them out LIGHT.*
> *They came in here doubting — send them out CERTAIN.*

[CRESCENDO ↑↑↑]

> *Because the God who brought them this far —*
> *is the SAME God who's going before them.*
> *The SAME God who closed that door.*
> *The SAME God who opened this one.*
> *The SAME God who kept them when they couldn't keep themselves.*

[PAUSE •••] [SLOW ↓]

> *We don't leave here the same.*
> *We leave here changed.*
> *We leave here covered.*
> *We leave here with the evidence of Your goodness on our lives.*

[WHISPER]

> *Thank You, Father.*
> *Thank You.*
> *Thank You.*

[PAUSE •••]

> *In the name of Jesus —*

[LET THEM RESPOND]

> *Somebody say — Amen.*"

> *"You may release hands.*

[PAUSE •••]

> *That person needed that.*
> *And so did you.*

> *Go in peace.*
> *Go in power.*
> *Go knowing that God is with you.*"

## DELIVERY REMINDERS

| Moment | Instruction |
|--------|------------|
| Opening setup | Slow. Reverent. Don't rush the altar call. Give it 45 seconds. |
| If no one comes | Don't panic. Call them to their feet. Make it corporate. |
| Movement 1 — Call | Quiet voice. Set the room. You're not performing — you're entering. |
| Movement 2 — Warfare | Build intentionally. Don't jump to crescendo early. Use the ladder. |
| Movement 3 — Personal | Drop back down. This is where the tears come. Let it breathe. |
| Movement 4 — Release | Build back up. Send them out victorious, not weeping. |
| The final "Thank You" | Say it like you mean it. Three times. Quietly. Let it land. |
| After the Amen | Give them a moment. Don't immediately go to announcements. |

## VARIATIONS

### SHORT VERSION (5 minutes — time-constrained service)
Use Movement 1 (Call) + Movement 4 (Release) only.
Skip Warfare and Personal — but do NOT skip the standing/hand-holding setup if no one comes forward.

### EXTENDED VERSION (15+ minutes — revival or special service)
Add personal prayer walks between Movements 2 and 3.
Allow time for individuals to pray aloud.
Let the music continue softly underneath throughout.

### COMMUNION SETTING
After Movement 1, transition to the communion elements.
Return to Movements 2–4 after the bread and cup.
