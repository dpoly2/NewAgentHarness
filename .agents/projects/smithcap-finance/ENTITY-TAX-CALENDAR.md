## 🔁 ESTIMATED TAX PAYMENT SCHEDULE

> Self-employment income from S2T Designs and any other business activity requires quarterly estimated tax payments to avoid underpayment penalties.

| Payment | Period Covers | Due Date | Estimated Amount |
|---------|--------------|----------|-----------------|
| Q1 2026 | Jan 1 – Mar 31 | Apr 15, 2026 | ✅ Passed |
| Q2 2026 | Apr 1 – May 31 | **Jun 16, 2026** | 🔴 Calculate now |
| Q3 2026 | Jun 1 – Aug 31 | Sep 15, 2026 | ⬜ TBD |
| Q4 2026 | Sep 1 – Dec 31 | Jan 15, 2027 | ⬜ TBD |

**How to calculate Q2 payment:**
- Take estimated 2026 total tax liability (federal)
- Subtract W-2 withholding (already paid by employer)
- Divide remainder by 4
- Pay via IRS Direct Pay at irs.gov/payments — no account needed

**Safe harbor rule:** Pay 100% of prior year (2025) tax liability spread across 4 quarters, or 90% of current year estimated liability — whichever is less. This avoids any underpayment penalty even if you owe at filing.
