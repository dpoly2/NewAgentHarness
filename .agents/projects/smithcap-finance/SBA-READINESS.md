## RISK FACTORS TO MONITOR

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| DTI too high | SBA denial | Pay down high-interest debt, avoid new personal debt |
| Credit score below 680 | SBA denial or higher rate | Dispute errors, reduce utilization, no new inquiries |
| No 2yr business history | SBA reluctance | Use CDFI bridge loan first; document existing S2T history |
| Insufficient collateral | SBA requires more equity | Use XFTC land partnership + personal assets as collateral |
| Tax liens or delinquencies | Automatic disqualification | Resolve any IRS issues before applying |
| Thin business credit | Slower approval | Start building D-U-N-S + Net-30 accounts now |
