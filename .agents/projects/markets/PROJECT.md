## Purpose

The Markets Division provides David with actionable stock and options intelligence — market research, macro analysis, technical setups, and specific picks with defined entry/exit criteria. This is NOT passive index investing (that's handled by finance-investment-manager). This is active capital allocation with a defined risk framework.

---

## Scope

- **Equity Research** — individual stock analysis, sector rotation, earnings plays
- **Options Strategy** — covered calls, cash-secured puts, spreads, income generation
- **Macro & Market Intelligence** — Fed watch, economic indicators, sector themes
- **Portfolio Management** — position sizing, risk management, P&L tracking
- **Weekly Picks Digest** — top 3–5 actionable ideas each week

---

## Risk Framework

| Parameter | Limit |
|-----------|-------|
| Max single position | 10% of liquid trading capital |
| Max sector concentration | 25% of liquid trading capital |
| Options risk per trade | Never risk more than 2% of account on one position |
| Stop-loss default | 8–10% below entry (unless thesis changes) |
| Weekly drawdown limit | 5% of trading account triggers review |

---

## Account Separation (IMPORTANT)

Trading capital is SEPARATE from:
- Emergency fund (HYSA — untouchable)
- 401(k) / Roth IRA (retirement — long-term index only)
- Business capital (Smith Capital entities)

Only "play money" — pre-defined trading capital — is used by this division.
