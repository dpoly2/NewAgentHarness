June 2026 | Issue 01 — Logic & Brotherhood

Five brothers — Marcus, Damon, Terrell, Jerome, and Calvin — each hold a different officer role in their graduate chapter: President, Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer, and Sergeant-at-Arms.

Use the clues below to figure out who holds which office:

  1. Marcus has never held an executive officer role — he is not the President or Vice President.
  2. Damon and Jerome are the two brothers who handle all the money — but only one of them is the Treasurer.
  3. Terrell calls roll at every meeting and keeps the official record of all chapter business.
  4. Jerome outranks Damon in the officer hierarchy.
  5. Calvin is responsible for order, discipline, and chapter protocol at every meeting.

🔍 Challenge:

Match each brother to his officer role. Can you solve it without guessing?
Hint: Start with what you know for certain before eliminating possibilities.
