## Overview
The Sigma Signal is a Phi Beta Sigma / Psi Beta Sigma 1914 chapter membership newsletter distributed via Constant Contact. It is designed to engage brothers aged 18–60 with curated content spanning fraternity news, culture, trivia, poetry, brain teasers, and membership development.

## Newsletter Sections (7 Core)
1. **Sigma BrainTeaser** — Moderately difficult; tailored for brothers 18–60; exported as standalone HTML block + answer key as separate file
2. **Chapter Spotlight** — Feature a chapter (collegiate or graduate); sourced from submissions or web/social research
3. **Poem** — Original or curated poetry aligned with Sigma values (brotherhood, service, scholarship)
4. **Trivia** — Phi Beta Sigma history, Black culture, or current events trivia; 3–5 questions per issue
5. **Membership Tips** — Practical fraternity life, civic engagement, financial health, or leadership development tips
6. **News & Updates** — National/regional fraternity news, announcements, upcoming events
7. **Brother Submissions** — Community-sourced content; brothers submit via designated email or form

---

## Content Sourcing Strategy
### Priority 1 — Submissions (Best)
- Brothers submit content to a designated inbox or form
- Submission categories: spotlights, poems, trivia questions, tips, photos

### Priority 2 — Research Fallback (When No Submissions)
- Scrape chapter websites and social media (Instagram, Facebook, chapter.phibetasigma.net)
- Pull from national Phi Beta Sigma communications
- Reference Constant Contact newsletters received at davidallensmith77@outlook.com
